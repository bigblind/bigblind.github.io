How to Make Your Website Accessible to People Who Use Screen Magnifiers

June 7, 2017 There’s a lot of content out there on how to make your website accessible. But I haven’t seen much on the subject of accessibility to users of screen magnifiers. I’m one of them, and I frequently run into annoying issues on the web. In this article, I’ll give some tips on how you can make your website more accessible to users of screen magnifying software. Disclaimers: This article is purely based on years of using the web with a screen magnifier.
